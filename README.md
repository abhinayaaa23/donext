# DoNext

### Plan Less. Execute More.

DoNext is an AI-powered workload forecasting and execution platform designed to help people stay ahead of deadlines, reduce planning stress, and focus on meaningful progress.

Unlike traditional task managers that simply organize tasks, DoNext helps users understand what lies ahead. By combining workload forecasting, intelligent task decomposition, risk-aware scheduling, and personalized guidance, the platform transforms overwhelming plans into clear next steps.

Built for students, researchers, creators, and curious minds, DoNext encourages thoughtful planning without sacrificing momentum.

---

## Why DoNext?

Most productivity tools answer:

> What tasks do I have?

DoNext answers:

> What should I do next?

The platform continuously evaluates workload distribution, approaching deadlines, task complexity, and planning patterns to surface actionable recommendations before small problems become stressful bottlenecks.

---

## Features

### Workload Forecasting

Visualize upcoming workload peaks before they happen.

DoNext analyzes deadlines and estimated effort to identify periods of high scheduling pressure, allowing users to make adjustments early.

### Intelligent Task Decomposition

Large projects can be difficult to start.

DoNext breaks ambitious goals into structured milestones and actionable focus sessions, making progress easier to begin and sustain.

### Risk-Aware Planning

Identify scheduling risks, deadline bottlenecks, and workload conflicts before they become overwhelming.

### Do Next Recommendations

Instead of presenting a long list of responsibilities, DoNext prioritizes the most meaningful next step.

### Nudge

Nudge is DoNext's planning guide.

Whether users need help estimating effort, reorganizing priorities, adjusting schedules, or recovering from a disrupted plan, Nudge provides conversational guidance tailored to the current workload.

### Planning Insights

Learn from workload patterns, planning habits, and estimation tendencies through personalized insights.

## Design Philosophy

DoNext was designed with a calm, editorial-inspired interface focused on reducing cognitive overload and helping users make confident planning decisions.
